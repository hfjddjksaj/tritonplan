## Description

```
TritonPlan brings back the course-planning calendar that UCSD's old WebReg had and the new Triton Student System (TSS) is missing.

Browse a course in TSS, click the "+ TritonPlan" button on any section, and it drops onto a clean weekly calendar — color-coded, with times, rooms, instructors, and final exams — so you can arrange your schedule and catch conflicts before you book.

FEATURES
• Weekly calendar view of your class times, with a live "now" line on today
• Automatic time-conflict detection and highlighting
• A Finals view — a date-ordered list plus a finals-week calendar — that flags overlapping final exams
• Switch a course's section combination to clear a conflict
• Share your plan by link, or export/import it as a file
• Jump from any calendar block back to that course in TSS

HOW IT WORKS
When you browse the Schedule of Classes, the TSS server already sends the course data to your browser. TritonPlan simply captures that data as it arrives and draws it on the planner. It never sends a request of its own and never automates TSS — it behaves exactly like normal browsing.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN
Your plan stays on your device, saved in your browser and in the share links you create. Nothing is collected or uploaded, and the planner site has no backend.

Open the planner: https://hfjddjksaj.github.io/tritonplan/
Open source on GitHub: https://github.com/hfjddjksaj/tritonplan

```
